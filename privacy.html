Privacy Policy

Last updated 14 June 2026

Futonix ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through futonix.com, how we use it, and your choices. By using this site you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

We only collect what you choose to give us:

Contact & enquiry formsName, work email, company, phone (if provided), project details, and how you heard about us.
Report sign-upsName, email and company when you request a report or resource.
Basic technical dataStandard server/CDN logs (e.g., IP, browser type) kept by our host for security and performance.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not ask for sensitive data (such as payment, government ID, or health information) through this site.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and scope, plan and deliver your project.
  • To send a resource or report you requested.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To meet legal, safety and contractual obligations.

Third parties we use

To run this site we rely on a few reputable service providers who process limited data on our behalf:

VercelWebsite hosting & delivery (server logs).
FormSubmitDelivers our form submissions to our inbox.
Google FontsServes the site typeface.

These providers have their own privacy practices. We share only what's needed to provide the service and never sell your data.

Cookies & tracking

This site uses minimal storage — primarily to remember preferences. We do not run advertising trackers. If we add analytics in future, we'll update this policy and honor your choices.

Data retention

We keep enquiry information only as long as needed to respond and, where relevant, to deliver and document a project, then dispose of it securely.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct or delete the personal information you've shared. Email projects@udgok.com and we'll respond promptly. Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under laws such as GDPR or CCPA.

Children

This site is intended for businesses and is not directed to children under 16.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above reflects the latest version.
