Who we build for

We love a project that's a turning point. Futonix builds for emerging businesses, growing operators and new locations — the people opening, expanding or modernizing. We bring big-firm discipline (PMP-led, OSHA-safe) to projects of every size, and we treat a single store with the same care as a 400,000 sq ft warehouse.

What we do

One team for the whole build.

Design, construction and technology under one roof — so there are no gaps to fall through and one number to call.

01

Design-Build

One contract for design and construction. We take you from napkin sketch to keys-in-hand, owning the outcome the whole way — faster, with fewer surprises and a single point of accountability.

02

General Contracting

Plans already drawn? We'll build them. Self-performing crews and trusted trades, tight scheduling, and clean, transparent management from groundbreak to punch list.

03

Construction Management

We run the job so you can run your business — coordinating trades, budget, schedule and quality, with live updates and no guesswork.

04

Preconstruction & Budgeting

Real numbers before you commit. Feasibility, value-engineering, permitting strategy and a budget you can trust — so there are no surprises when the crews show up.

05

Technology & Automation

The part most builders hand off, we own: structured data cabling, access control, security cameras (including facial recognition), PA & telecom, and network & lighting controls — designed in, not bolted on.

06

Renovation & Tenant Improvement

From a historic 80-unit remodel to a fast retail or clinic fit-out, we modernize and transform existing space with minimal disruption to your operation.
