What is Futonix?

Futonix is a U.S. design-build and automation firm delivering healthcare, warehouse, commercial and multifamily projects end-to-end — plus the technology behind them: data, security, access control and building controls. One accountable team handles design, construction and automation under a single contract, removing the handoffs that cost traditional projects time and money.
