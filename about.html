Why Futonix

One team. Zero gaps.

Most projects lose months — and millions — in the handoffs between designers, builders and integrators. Futonix removes the handoffs entirely. We design it, we build it, we automate it. You hold one number accountable: ours.

Our approach

Fast-forward, never rushed.

Speed comes from clarity, not corner-cutting. By modelling every system in BIM and self-performing critical trades, we compress timelines while raising the quality bar. The result: buildings that open sooner and run better.

We're rooted in Oklahoma and work across the U.S. — and as a self-performing team we're resourced to mobilize to new markets, whether that's a healthcare build-out, a distribution warehouse or the technology and automation behind a commercial space.
