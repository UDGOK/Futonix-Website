Northgate Fulfilment Hub

Rotterdam, NetherlandsCompleted 2024Design-BuildIndustrial · Distribution42,000 m²

Last updated 14 June 2026

Key takeaways

  • Futonix designed and built a 42,000 m² automated distribution centre in Rotterdam on a single design-build contract.
  • The facility integrates chilled and frozen cold-chain zones, 38 dock doors and an AS/RS-ready structural grid.
  • Rooftop solar and smart metering make the building net-zero ready.

What did Futonix deliver at Northgate?

Futonix delivered a large-span automated fulfilment centre turnkey. The integrated design-build model aligned the structural grid, racking, dock automation and refrigeration as one engineered system rather than separately bid packages.

The challenge

The operator needed high-throughput capacity with temperature-controlled zones and headroom for future automation — delivered to a fixed go-live for peak season, on reclaimed ground requiring careful structural engineering.

The solution

A long-span steel structure was engineered AS/RS-ready, with chilled and frozen envelopes detailed for thermal performance and continuous monitoring. Energy systems — rooftop solar, LED and smart metering — were designed in from day one rather than retrofitted.
