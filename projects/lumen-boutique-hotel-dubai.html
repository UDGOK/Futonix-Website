Lumen Boutique Hotel

Dubai, UAECompleted 2024Design-Build & Fit-outHospitality · Lifestyle hotel88 keys

Last updated 14 June 2026

Key takeaways

  • Futonix delivered an 88-key lifestyle hotel in Dubai — design, build and full interior fit-out under one contract.
  • Every room runs smart-room automation (lighting, climate and access) integrated at handover.
  • Front-of-house atmosphere was balanced against back-of-house efficiency to protect operating margins.

What did Futonix deliver at Lumen?

Futonix delivered the hotel end-to-end: architecture, construction, MEP, commercial kitchens, interior fit-out and smart-building automation on one design-build contract — so guest experience and operations were engineered together.

The challenge

A lifestyle operator wanted a distinctive guest experience without sacrificing operational efficiency or energy performance in a demanding climate — delivered to an opening date tied to the season.

The solution

Interiors, MEP and automation were coordinated in one model. Smart-room controls were specified for guest comfort and energy savings, while back-of-house and commercial kitchens were laid out for throughput and compliance.
