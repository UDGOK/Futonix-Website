Security at Futonix

Last updated 14 June 2026

The short version

We keep this site and the information you share with us secure with HTTPS everywhere, reputable hosting, careful form handling, and a no-nonsense approach to data minimization — we only collect what we need.

Website & data in transit

  • The entire site is served over HTTPS/TLS — no mixed content.
  • Hosted on Vercel's global, security-hardened edge network.
  • It's a static site: there's no public database or admin panel to breach.

Form & enquiry handling

  • Form submissions are transmitted securely and delivered to a monitored inbox.
  • We include spam protection on our forms.
  • We never request payment details, passwords, or government IDs through the site.
  • We practice data minimization and retain enquiry data only as long as needed (see our Privacy Policy).

Security on the projects we build

Security isn't just our website — it's a service. Futonix designs and installs the physical and digital security layer of the buildings we deliver: access control, surveillance cameras (including facial recognition), structured data networks, and building controls, engineered to protect people, assets and operations. Our work is delivered OSHA-compliant and PMP-led.

Responsible disclosure

If you believe you've found a security issue with this website, please tell us before disclosing it publicly. Email projects@udgok.com with details and we'll respond quickly. We appreciate good-faith reports.
