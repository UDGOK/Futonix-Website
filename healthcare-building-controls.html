Healthcare · Technology

Smart building controls for healthcare.

Last updated 14 June 2026

The short answer

Healthcare buildings demand controls that are secure, compliant and always-on. Futonix designs and integrates the technology layer of clinical spaces — access control, video security, structured data, environmental and lighting controls — as part of the build, so patient safety, privacy and uptime are engineered in, not retrofitted.

What we integrate

01

Access control & door hardware

Role-based entry for clinical, admin and restricted areas; audit trails that support privacy and after-hours security.

02

Video security & surveillance

Camera coverage for entries, waiting areas and sensitive zones — high-resolution, retention-ready, optional facial recognition.

03

Structured data & networks

Clinical-grade cabling and network backbone for EHR, imaging, devices and VoIP — designed for reliability and growth.

04

Environmental & lighting controls

HVAC, lighting and monitoring tuned for comfort, energy efficiency and consistent clinical conditions.

Engineered for compliance

Clinical environments carry obligations around privacy, safety and infection control. We engineer the technology with those obligations in mind — supporting HIPAA-aware access/audit practices, life-safety and egress requirements, and the documentation your authority having jurisdiction expects. (We coordinate with your compliance and clinical teams; specific certifications depend on facility type and jurisdiction.)

It's the same integrated approach we brought to healthcare build-outs like Paradigm Oral Health and The Eye Institute. Learn more about design-build automation or our healthcare sector work.
